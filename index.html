    About us

    FANSHAWE
    Interactive

    Discover Our Program and Its Industry-Ready Preparation Unlock Your Future in Digital Creativity with Us! Dive into digital design, development, and innovation with Fanshawe College's two-year Interactive Media Design program. Explore how our curriculum equips you with the skills needed to excel in the industry.

    We Design

    Our program emphasizes graphic, motion, and video design, empowering you to craft visually captivating multimedia content that resonates with audiences. You'll gain proficiency in industry-standard tools to create engaging designs that communicate powerful messages.

    We Code

    In our coding component, you'll master the development of dynamic web pages using CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. This hands-on training will enable you to transform your design concepts into interactive online experiences that enhance user engagement.

    We Build

    You will construct innovative web projects that adhere to industry standards by integrating your design and coding skills. This comprehensive approach prepares you to create dynamic websites that provide seamless user experiences and showcase your creative vision.

    I invite you to discover how our Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College equips students with the essential skills for success in the digital industry. With a focus on web design, coding, and animation, our comprehensive curriculum and state-of-the-art facilities ensure you are well-prepared to seize exciting career opportunities and stay ahead in a rapidly evolving landscape.
    Marco Deluca

    Program Coordinator
